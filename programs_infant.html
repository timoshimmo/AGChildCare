Programs

Infants

Our programs for infants are setup in such a way to keep them healthy, happy and active. They learn new things in a fun and exciting manner and receive the outmost care and love from our experienced teachers/staffs

They are provided meals daily and milk every 3 hours for their nutritional needs and enough nap time for their growth. They also have fun activities during the day like sensory play, guidance, beginners walking and so much more. We do our best to provide the essential needs of each infant daily and to keep them happy.

Our programs are mostly setup for morning and evening sessions
