Services

What We Offer

Our services are open to infants and toodlers between 3 months and 4 years. We have a very favourable schedule for working families. You can drop in at any time.

We are open from mondays to saturdays except public holidays for infants and toodlers. Our services include:

  • 1 teacher for 4 infants.
  • 1 teacher for 8 toodlers
  • Limited quota for better care
  • Provision of food and toiletries
  • Daily activities
  • Learning through practice, repetition and play

Our facilities

We ensure to provide the best environment for learning, resting, playing, eating and so on. We are thorough about cleanliness and healthy living.

Our centers have been structures to fully cater to all the needs of the children and also the teachers. Our centers are equipped with:

  • Fully airconditioned
  • Indoor playground for Little Kids
  • Professional & Friendly Staffs
  • Mini library
  • Separate infant room
  • Secure door lock system
  • 24 hours CCTV for safety
  • Fire safety for maximum protection

Important notice

You can drop by at any time without any need for reservations but for infants below 18 months, a reservation is required.
